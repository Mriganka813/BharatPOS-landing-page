Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why should I use POS Software?

    A POS system software can help you automize your business and ease invoiced generation, inventory management, employee management, etc. The cash management becomes easy. POS software saves a lot of time and is much more efficient than a traditional cash register. It also enhances the customer experience; helps you keep track of expiration and prevents theft.

  • How do I sign up in the app?

    Open the app> Go to sign in> Enter details> Enter phone no.> Enter the OTP> Enter password> Confirm password. And you’re all set.

  • Do BharatPOS needs any extra hardware?

    No. BharatPOS doesn’t require you buy any additional hardware. Hence, once you’ve invested in buying BharatPOS, you don’t have to invest in expensive hardware again.

  • Can we share purchase bill with customers?

    Yes. You can share the bills with customers via WhatsApp too without saving the contact.

  • Can I analyse customer data with a POS?

    Yes. Our POS is integrated with AI which collects data about customer buying behaviour. You can analyse the data and make amends to your business to generate more profits.
    You can also access daily purchase reports on the ‘Repot’ section of the app.

  • Can I connect the app to various receipt printers?

    Yes. You can connect BharatPOS with wireless bluetooth/wifi printers.
