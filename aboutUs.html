About Us

BharatPOS developed by Magicstep solution private limited is the first Indian software to bring its one-of-a-kind AI integrated POS + e-commerce marketplace.

With this AI integration, we look forward to revolutionizing the way small and medium retailers do business.

  • We believe every retailer deserves to have their fair share in the online marketplace which is currently being dominated by the large retailer because they have a huge buying power and can sell to the customer at a price that isn’t even purchasing price for small retailers.
  • We want to lend a hand to small retailers and share the information collected by the AI about customers’ purchasing behaviour so that the sellers can make informed decisions about which products are selling more and which one is not in demand.
  • With this AI integrated software, we want to empower small and medium sellers, make them more efficient and rise them back to the status that they once had in the market.
